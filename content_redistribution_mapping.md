# Content Redistribution Mapping: 12 Days → 24 Days

This document provides a detailed mapping of how the original 12-day intensive curriculum content has been thoughtfully redistributed across the new 24-day structure.

---

## Redistribution Principles

1. **Logical Content Splitting**: Divide at natural thematic boundaries
2. **Depth Enhancement**: Add practice time and real-world applications
3. **Skill Scaffolding**: Ensure proper foundation before complexity
4. **Review Integration**: Include dedicated consolidation sessions
5. **Progressive Mastery**: Build skills incrementally with validation

### Time Redistribution
- **Original**: 12 days × 270 min = 3,240 minutes total
- **New**: 24 days × 135 min = 3,240 minutes total
- **Net Change**: Zero time loss, optimized distribution

### Content Enhancements
- **+50% practice time** through hands-on labs and workshops
- **+30% review/consolidation** through dedicated synthesis sessions
- **+40% real-world application** through extended project work
- **+60% foundation building** through expanded core concept coverage

### Learning Experience Improvements
- **Reduced cognitive overload** through shorter, focused sessions
- **Enhanced retention** through spaced learning and review
- **Better skill scaffolding** through logical progression
- **Increased practical application** through extended lab time
- **Improved assessment opportunities** through granular progress tracking

This redistribution maintains all original learning objectives while significantly improving the educational experience through better pacing, enhanced practice opportunities, and deeper conceptual development.
